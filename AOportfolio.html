A Little Bit About Me

Hey there! My name is Anahi Ojeda. My dad was the one who chose my name, and I kind of almost resented him for it growing up. Almost everyone I meet always has a difficult time pronouncing my name, let alone remember it. That's how it's always been growing up. It used to bother me when I was younger when I moved away from the city and into the suburbs, but it doesn't anymore. I just appreciate it when people at least try. I've researched the origin of the name "Anahi", I found out it has Persian roots and means "immortal". I think that's pretty badass.
