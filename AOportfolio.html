A Little Bit About Me

Hey there! My name is Anahi Ojeda. My dad was the one who chose my name, and I kind of almost resented him for it growing up. Almost everyone I meet always has a difficult time pronouncing my name, let alone remember it. That's how it's always been growing up. It used to bother me when I was younger when I moved away from the city and into the suburbs, but it doesn't anymore. I just appreciate it when people at least try. I've researched the origin of the name "Anahi", I found out it has Persian roots and means "immortal". I think that's pretty badass.


I was born in Chicago and grew up in Humboldt Park. Both of my parents immigrated from Mexico, making me first generation Mexican-American. I have a huge family with a BUNCH of cousins; over 30.


I big hobby of mine is painting. I started off with just drawing and then picked up watercolors more seriously about 7 years ago. So I have a pretty cool colletion of art supplies. I realized the reason I like painting so much, is because it is truly the only time my brain shuts EVERYTHING out. I do not think about drama. I do not have ruminating thoughts. It's just me in my own little world with colors.


I Love Traveling !

I know it probably sounds a bit cliche or eye-rolling to hear someone say they love to travel, but the reason why I love it is because it has truly opened my mind and eyes and has made me a better person in a way. I am grateful in way I cannot begin to describe for the experiences I've had with the people from the countries I've been to.
Here is a list of the countries I've visited:

  • Mexico
  • Canada
  • Italy
  • Austria
  • Germany
  • Slovenia
  • France
  • England
  • San Marino (it's a small country in the middle Italy, they speak Italian, but they don't consider themselves Italian...)
  • Greece
  • Jordan (by far my favorite!)

It's mainly been in Europe and a bunch different places in Mexico/Italy, but I want to move more East in the near future.


But my heart will always stay in Mexico where my grandparents are from.

Random/weird/potentially cool fun fact: I am ambidextrous!
